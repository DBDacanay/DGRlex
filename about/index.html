About this dictionary

The Tłı̨chǫ Online Dictionary is a bilingual dictionary of Tłı̨chǫ and English.

It has been constructed to assist both fluent speakers and learners of the language to access Tłı̨chǫ vocabulary online, as well as to serve as a repository for general language information.

To facilitate this, the Tłı̨chǫ Online Dictionary has many novel features to assist the user in finding relevant entries. You can read about how to use these features, and how to use the dictionary more generally, here.

This dictionary contains over 11500 entries, provided by speakers from across Tłı̨chǫ lands. Most of these entries come from the 1996 print edition of Tłı̨chǫ Yatıı̀ Enı̨htł’è - Dogrib Dictionary, however, Tłı̨chǫ words and phrases from a wide variety of other sources have also been incorporated into this dictionary. A full list of the sources used may be found here.

Just as Tłı̨chǫ is a living language, the Tłı̨chǫ Online Dictionary is a living document. We are constantly adding new entries, updating definitions, and providing new supplementary information, and welcome any additional feedback. You can suggest new entries here, or send us an email at daniel.dacanay @ tlicho.ca.
